The Problem & Opportunity

The Intelligence Gap in Crypto Markets

  • Retail Blindness: 90% of retail traders only use technical indicators, ignoring social sentiment and whale moves.
  • The Cost Barrier: Professional sentiment terminals (Santiment/LumiBot) cost $5,000+/year.
  • The Privacy Risk: Cloud trading bots own your data and strategy patterns.
  • Opportunity: Use local LLMs (Mistral/Llama) to provide institutional power for $0.
